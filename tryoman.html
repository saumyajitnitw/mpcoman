Muscat Petroleum Consultancy (MPC) is a specialized consultancy firm, founded by a group of professional integrity engineers. It focuses it’s services on Asset & Pipeline Integrity consultancy. The founders of the Company possess more than 50 years of collective experience in pipeline and asset integrity in the oil and gas sector.

Demand of a such wealth of knowledge is significantly increasing in oil and gas industry, particularly in middle east, as the economical life of assets approaches their ends. The founders aimed to make this knowledge available to industry by establishing (MPC) consultancy services.
